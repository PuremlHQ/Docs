## Key features

Here are some of the key features that make Pureml the go-to solution for your ML workflow:

**Policy Management**: Create, store, and manage easy to understand policies and procedures across the organization to align overall goals and objectives.

**Risk assessment**: PureML makes it easy for organization to take decisions by identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks.

**Version Control**: With Pureml, you can easily version your models and datasets, ensuring that you have a clear record of all changes made throughout the development process.

**Commit Process**: Pureml incorporates a review commit process that helps you ensure that only the best and most reliable models and datasets are shipped to your customers.

**Packaging**: Whether you need to package your models into Docker, Gradio, or FastAPI, Pureml has you covered. Our platform allows you to easily package your models in the format that works best for your specific needs.

**Data Lineage**: Pureml gives you full visibility into your datasets' lineage, making it easy to trace back any issues that may arise during development or deployment.

**Testing**: Pureml includes testing for ML, making it easy to ship models reliably to your customers. With Pureml, you can be confident that your models will work as expected every time.

Overall, Pureml is the ideal platform for teams looking to streamline their ML workflow. Whether you're working on small or large-scale projects, Pureml has the features you need to succeed. Try it out today and experience the power of Pureml for yourself!
