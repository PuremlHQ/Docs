## Fill Forms

To fill out forms, follow the steps below:

1. Check the Controls tab and click on "Forms." ![Controls Tab](../images/dashboard.png)


2. In the Forms section, you can filter the forms using Policy Pack and Models. ![Filter Documents](../images/forms1.png)

3. Click on the Policy name to open a new page.
4. On the new page, you can fill the form and save the changes. ![Upload Document](../images/forms2.png)
