## Answering Questionnaire

To answer the questionnaire, follow the steps below:

1. Check the Controls tab and click on "Questionaire." ![Controls Tab](../images/dashboard.png)


2. In the Questionaire section, you can filter the questions using Policy Pack and Models. ![Filter Documents](../images/questionnaire.png)

3. Click on the Policy name to open a new page.
4. On the new page, you can answer the question and save the changes. ![Upload Document](../images/questionnaire2.png)
