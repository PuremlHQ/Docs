## Uploading Documents 

To apply required documents in SuperAlign, follow these steps:

1. Check the Controls tab and click on "Documents." ![Controls Tab](../images/dashboard.png)


2. In the Documents section, you can filter the documents using Policy Pack and Models. ![Filter Documents](../images/documents1.png)

3. Click on the Policy name to open a new page.
4. On the new page, you can upload the file, add comments, and change the status as needed. ![Upload Document](../images/documents2.png)


<Info>
You can upload documents of type pdf
</Info>
